Paw Patrol Inc.(all rights reserved by the regd. trademark) was established by a team of professionals in Jan 2023, due to the absence of a robust digital platform for animals in Pakistan. Paw Patrol is Pakistan's first business entity that offers pet products, veterinary services and animal adoption services under one roof.

Paw Patrol is Pakistan's first company with an in-country facility. All of our pet products, including food and accessories, are manufactured at our production factories and/or plants in Pakistan. This ensures state-of-the-art processes, premium ingredients and quality-inspected items. 10% of the proceeds of all products sold by Paw Patrol, directly fund hand-picked animal welfare societies of Pakistan.
